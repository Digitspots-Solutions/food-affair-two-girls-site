About Us

Food Affair / Two Girls is a dedicated food manufacturing company based in Bwari, Nigeria. We specialize in producing high-quality food products that meet the needs of Nigerian families and businesses. Our commitment to excellence, food safety standards, and authentic taste has made us a trusted name in the industry.

Operating from our modern facility in Kubwa, we combine traditional recipes with contemporary manufacturing practices to deliver products that consistently exceed expectations. Every item that leaves our facility reflects our passion for quality and our dedication to nourishing our community.
